Modifications of SOHCAHTOA

\[ \text{sin} \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}} = \frac{\text{opposite}}{1} = \text{opposite} \, (\text{y coord}) \]

\[ \text{cos} \theta = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}} = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{1} = \text{adjacent} \, (\text{x coord}) \]

\[ \text{tan} \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}} = \frac{\text{sin}\theta}{\text{cos}\theta} \]

The values of \( \text{sin}\theta \) and \( \text{cos}\theta \) are inversely proportional.

As \( \lim_{\theta \to 0} \) (or theta approaches to zero), the value of \( \text{cos}\theta \) decreases, and \( \text{sin}\theta \) increases.
