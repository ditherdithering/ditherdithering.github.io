Organisms get oxygen and releases carbon dioxide from the body.

Helps cells make energy to do work.

Breathing is controlled by the Medulla Oblongata.

Why is oxygen important:

  • Blood carries oxygen throughout the body to maintain proper functioning of our body parts
  • Sustains essential biological processes like Cellular Respiration
