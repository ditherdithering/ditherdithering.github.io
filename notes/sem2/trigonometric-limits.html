Trigonometric Limits Examples

a

\[ \lim_{x\to\frac{\pi}{6}} \frac{2 \sin x}{\sqrt3 \sec x - \cot^2 x} \]

b

\[ \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{\sin 3x}{\sin 5x} = \frac{\lim_{x\to 0} 3\left(\frac{\sin 3x}{3x}\right)}{\lim_{x\to 0} 5\left(\frac{\sin 5x}{5x}\right)} = \frac{3\lim_{x\to 0} \left(\frac{\sin 3x}{3x}\right)}{5\lim_{x\to 0} \left(\frac{\sin 5x}{5x}\right)} = \frac{(3)(1)}{(5)(1)} = \frac{3}{5} \]

c

\[ \lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\cos x - 1}{2 \sin x} = \frac{-(1 - \cos x)}{2 \sin x} = \frac{\frac{- 1 + \cos x}{x}}{\frac{2 \sin x}{x}} = \frac{\lim_{x\to 0} \frac{- 1 + \cos x}{x}}{\lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2 \sin x}{x}} = \frac{0}{2 \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}} = \frac{0}{2(1)} = 0 \]
