Freedom of Choice

We are all making a choice. Everything we do or say in life is a choice.

Aristotle distinguished man as the only rational animal. That means:

  • Humans alone possess the faculty of reason
  • Reason gives us awareness of our freedom of choice John Kavanaugh - Freedom of choice comes with its ambiguity, deliberation, irrevocability, and the terror of making choices.

Be grounded in reasoning.
