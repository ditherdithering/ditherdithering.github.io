Morality

  • narrowly means the moral principles of culture, society, and/or individuals
  • generally refers to set of moral beliefs and practices of culture, society, or individuals, religion, or code or system of moral values, principles, or values
  • set from individuals, groups, societys, or culture's moral standards
  • guides human behaviour

Moral Standards

  • determines actions if they are acceptable or not
  • not following moral standards are considered immoral or unacceptable

Ethics

  • set of principles of moral behaviour of a social group/society towards others based on basic social values
  • its the science of morality as a social phenomena that is expressed in concrete human actions within the rules that society follows as a guiding light of civilization
  • as a branch of [[PHILO 101 Dashboard|Philosophy]], its an examination of basis and source of morality, aka criterias
