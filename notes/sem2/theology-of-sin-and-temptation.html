Sins

Abuse or misuse of freedom

Should a man sin, their freedom is strained, and if freedom is strained, dignity is strained, and if dignity is strained, virtues is strained, creating bad habits.

Rejection of God and of our true selves

Can also be:

  • Refusing God's love
  • Rejecting to follow one's own conscience's call towards the good
  • Breaking God's loving covenant

Sin in Old Testament Models or 3 K's

  • Missing the Mark, "Kulang"
  • Disorder of character, "Kolokoy"
  • Rebelling against God, "Kalaban"

Sin in New Testament Models or 3 H's

  • A spiral that enslaves us, "Hawa"
  • A sickness, "Hina"
  • An addiction that weakens, "Humaling"
