Mammalian Heart

Human Heart

  • Hollow, about the size of a clenched fist
  • Consists of cardiac muscles
  • Surrounded by pericardium
  • Has four chambers

    Parts of the Heart

    Heartbeat

    One contraction (systole) + One relaxation (diastole) = One heartbeat

    Nodal Tissues

    Sinoatrial Node (SAN)
    Also known as natural pacemaker.

Located in the upper wall of the right atrium, near the Superior VC entrance.

Atrioventricular Node (AVN)

Also known as the gatekeeper of electrical signals.

Located in the lower part of the right atrium, near the septum.

Ensures that there is a slight delay in the transmission of electrical impulses.
