  • Art, coming from Latin word ars meaning skill

Definitions

  • Product of man's need to express himself
  • Elevated form of communication
  • Can transform ideas into products that later on concretizes the idea in mind
  • Can reveal many things about the artist and his environments, w/ intent or not
  • Concerns with the communication of certain ideas and feelings by means of a sensuous medium, words, & expression.

Principles

  1. Man creates art
    • Art takes its source from nature
  2. Art exists with man
    • As long man thinks, art can be created
  3. Art imitates
    • Mimesis: the act of representing or imitating reality in art
  4. Art interprets
    • Reality is interpreted by the artist with the art
  5. Art expresses & communicates
    • Art can be made to express its main point, and subtly or blatantly communicates it
  6. Art affects, delights, and evokes a reaction.
    • The reaction or affect must be intended
  7. Art processes an experience
    • An artist makes the meaning, it is up for the viewer to deduce the meaning of the art
  8. Art processes an order
    • Patterns, Sequences, Rules, Symmetry
    • Makes sure the people you show art align to your order
  9. Art embodies values & beauty
    • Related to order
    • Appreciates with the harmony, balance, or beauty

Elements

  1. Line
    • Collection of points
  2. Shape
    • Collection of lines
  3. Form
    • 3D Depth, collection of shapes
  4. Space
    • The internal and external area of a shape
  5. Value
    • Brightness
  6. Colour
    • Chromatic quality
  7. Texture
    • Make it how it feels like
