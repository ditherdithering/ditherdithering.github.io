\[ (\text{a}) \hspace{2em} \frac{\cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta}{\cos \theta + \sin \theta} = \cos \theta - \sin \theta \]

\[ (\text{b}) \hspace{2em} \sin^2 \theta = \cos^2 \theta + 1 \]

Trig Identities

Ex. 1

\[ (\text{a}) \hspace{2em} \frac{\tan \theta \cos \theta}{\sin \theta} \]

The Solution

\[ \frac{\frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta} \cos \theta}{\sin \theta} \] Expound \( \tan \theta \), cancel out the cosines, \( \sin \div \sin = 1 \).

Ex. 1 (b)

\[ (\text{b}) \hspace{2em} \frac{\cos \theta}{\cot \theta} \]

The Solution

\[ \frac{\cos \theta}{\frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}} \] \[ \cos \theta \left(\frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}\right) \] \[ = \sin \theta \]

Ex. 3

\[ \sin \theta = -\frac{4}{5} \hspace{0.5em} \text{and} \hspace{0.25em} \cos \theta > 0 \]

The Solution

\[ \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1 \] \[ \cos^2 \theta = 1 - \left(-\frac{4}{5}\right)^2 \] \[ \sqrt{\cos^2 \theta} = \sqrt{0.36} \] \[ \cos \theta = 0.6 = \frac{3}{5} \] \[ \tan \theta = \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}, \hspace{2em} \tan \theta = \frac{-\frac{4}{5}}{\frac{3}{5}}, \hspace{2em} \tan \theta = -\frac{4}{3} \] \[ \csc \theta = \frac{1}{\sin \theta}, \hspace{2em} \csc \theta = -\frac{5}{4} \] \[ \sec \theta = \frac{1}{\cos \theta}, \hspace{2em} \sec \theta = \frac{5}{3} \] \[ \cot \theta = \frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}, \hspace{2em} \cot \theta = -\frac{3}{4} \]

Ex. 4

\[ \sec \theta = \frac{5}{2}, \hspace{1.25em} \tan \theta < 0 \]

The identities tell us that \( \theta \) is in quadrant 4, implying that \( \sin \theta < 0 \) and \( \cos \theta > 0 \).

The Solution

\[ \cos \theta = \frac{1}{\sec \theta} = \frac{2}{5} \] \[ \cos^2 \theta + \sin^2 \theta = 1, \hspace{1em} \sin^2 \theta = 1 - \cos^2 \theta, \hspace{2em} \sin \theta = \sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{2}{5}\right)^2}, \hspace{2em} \sin \theta = -\frac{\sqrt{21}}{5} \] \[ \tan \theta = \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}, \hspace{2em} \tan \theta = \frac{-\frac{\sqrt{21}}{5}}{\frac{2}{5}}, \hspace{2em} \tan \theta = -\frac{\sqrt{21}}{2} \] \[ \csc \theta = \frac{1}{\sin \theta}, \hspace{2em} \csc \theta = -\frac{5}{\sqrt{21}}, \hspace{2em} \csc \theta = -\frac{5\sqrt{21}}{21} \] \[ \cot \theta = \frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}, \hspace{2em} \cot \theta = -\frac{2}{\sqrt{21}}, \hspace{2em} \cot \theta = -\frac{2\sqrt{21}}{21} \]
